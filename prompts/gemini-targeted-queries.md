# Targeted Gemini Follow-up Queries

Run these as separate prompts. They're designed to fill specific gaps.

---

## Query 1: High-Volume Sender Domains

The previous analysis only found 7-10 rules. I need more.

Look through my entire email history and give me every sender domain that has sent me more than 10 emails total. For each domain, tell me:

1. The domain
2. Approximate total email count
3. Whether ALL emails from that domain are the same type (consistent) or mixed
4. If consistent: what category (use these categories: Scouting, Kids, Financial, Shopping, Marketing, Newsletters, Career, Government, Travel, Security)
5. If mixed: what types you see

Sort by email count, highest first. I want the long tail — even domains with just 10-15 emails. Give me the full list, not a summary. Use a table.

---

## Query 2: Google Workspace Notifications

Search my email for messages from Google — specifically:
- Google Drive sharing notifications
- Google Docs comment notifications  
- Google Calendar invites and updates
- Google Groups messages
- Google Forms responses
- Any other automated Google Workspace notifications

For each type, tell me:
1. What sender address it comes from
2. Approximate volume
3. Whether these typically need my attention or are ignorable

---

## Query 3: What Am I Missing?

Look at my email from the last 2 years. Find automated emails that do NOT fit cleanly into any of these categories:

Scouting, Kids, Financial, Shopping, Marketing, Newsletters, Career, Government, Travel, Security

What's left over? Are there patterns I'm not seeing? Any category I should add?

Give specific examples of emails that would be hard to classify.
